5" Beginner Quads

Easy to build, cheap, and durable to learn on

Specced out to be as easy to put together, affordable and plentiful parts, and easy to solder electronics. I haven't included radio systems, as those are largely up to preference, and budget
Prices may depend on where you source the parts. (Prices and specs are sourced from the manufacturers website when possible)
These are not affiliate links for your, and my convenience.

Most of the flight controllers listed have complete stack variants listed, unless specifically mentioned. Just so you don't have to go get the ESC separately, also ensuring compatibility, and better ease of use.
For the proper function of multilinks, you may need to enable popups
